Pasta with Meat Sauce

Description

The easiest way to make a delicious, nostalgic, and home-cooked meal is to make pasta with meat sauce. This dish has so much flexibility because the type of pasta, the type of meat, and the type of sauce can be so easily substituted.

This recipe will use ground beef, red sauce, and spaghetti, however, using alfredo sauce will lead to a very creamy pasta, and using ground turkey is my preferred method. Nonetheless, we shall begin.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • ground beef
  • red sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion powder
  • minced garlic
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring 4 quarts of water for every pound of pasta to a boil and add salt to water
  2. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until al dente and strain in a colander
  3. Set the heat to medium-high and add olive oil and ground beef to the pot
  4. Add salt, pepper, onion powder, and minced garlic to the meat and continue to cook until brown
  5. Add in red sauce and set heat on low, simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Add back in pasta, stir to combine, and serve
