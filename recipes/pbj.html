Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is the most classic and infamous meal. It requires 3 simple ingredients: bread, peanut butter, and jelly. In its simple form it is a sandwich made for children, however, it can very easily be enhanced to new heights with the addition of a few small ingredients and adjustments.

By using a more exquisite nut butter, such as almond butter or sunflower seed butter, in combination with a light dusting of cinnamon powder, a drizzle of honey, and some time spend on a pan after being lightly buttered, a transformation occurs. The simple PB&J takes on new attributes and ascends to the immortal realm of sandwiches, alongside such as the blt, the tuna salad sandwich, and the hot pastrami on rye to name a few.

Ingredients (simple vs. fancy)

  • Peanut Butter (Trader Joe's Almond Butter)
  • Jelly of choice (Grape is the superior flavor)
  • Bread of choice (Sourdough or White Bread is preferred)
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread and spread the nut butter on one side and the jelly on the other
  2. Sprinkle cinnamon and drizzle honey on either piece of bread
  3. Combine the pieces of bread and butter the top side, place
  4. Place butter-side-down on a pan and set heat to medium
  5. Wait several minutes until the bread has toasted, butter the other side, which is on top now, and flip.
  6. Enjoy!
