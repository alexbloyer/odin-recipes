Carne Asada Tacos

Description

This recipe details the step-by-step process of making some delicious and authentic Carne Asada Tacos! They're very easy to make and flavorful. You will love these tacos! You can use top sirloin, New York, filet mignon or similar cuts of beef.

These are perfect for a simple yet complex dinner that will leave you wanting more. It takes about 30 minutes of preparation and 45 minutes of cooking, and it serves 6 people.
