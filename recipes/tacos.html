Carne Asada Tacos

Description

This recipe details the step-by-step process of making some delicious and authentic Carne Asada Tacos! They're very easy to make and flavorful. You will love these tacos! You can use top sirloin, New York, filet mignon or similar cuts of beef.

These are perfect for a simple yet complex dinner that will leave you wanting more. It takes about 30 minutes of preparation and 45 minutes of cooking, and it serves 6 people.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • corn tortillas
  • lime
  • diced onion
  • tomatoes
  • tomatillos
  • jalapenos
  • oil

Steps

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper, squeeze lime juice, coat evenly, cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes
  2. Puree tomatillos and jalapenos in a food processor or blender and cook in a neutral cooking oil
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet and saute 1/3 of the beef at a time for 1 minute, and heat tortillas in an oven or microwave
  4. Place two tortillas on top of each other and add meat, salsa, onions, and tomatoes.
